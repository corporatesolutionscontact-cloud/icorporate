About iCorporate QMS

A consortium of experienced professionals dedicated to strengthening quality systems and building institutions of lasting excellence.

Our Philosophy

Driving Institutional Excellence Through Compliance

ICorporate QMS is a professional consulting organization dedicated to helping institutions and businesses strengthen their quality systems and achieve internationally recognized standards. We work across the full spectrum of quality management — from initial system design to final accreditation — ensuring our clients are thoroughly prepared to meet the highest regulatory and compliance benchmarks.

Our core philosophy is that improving the quality of an institution ultimately contributes to the growth and development of society. By helping organizations build structured systems, strong governance frameworks, and continuous improvement cultures, we create institutions that deliver lasting value.

ICorporate QMS operates as a consortium of experienced professionals drawn from diverse sectors, bringing together deep expertise in quality management, education, healthcare standards, food safety, and organizational development.

Our Mission

To elevate organizational performance through standard-driven best practices, robust quality management systems, and continuous improvement methodologies.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted compliance consulting partner for institutions pursuing ISO certification, institutional accreditation, and quality-driven growth.

Industry Expertise

Our Focus Areas

Education

We support schools, colleges, and higher education institutions through QCI accreditations, NABET certifications, NAAC accreditation, and ISO 21001 implementation.

Services include documentation development, policy frameworks, faculty training programs, and institutional quality improvement systems.

Healthcare

We assist hospitals, diagnostic centers, and healthcare organizations in implementing robust quality systems and achieving NABH accreditation and NABL certification. Our consulting focuses on clinical governance frameworks, operational procedures, and quality documentation aligned with national healthcare compliance standards.

Manufacturing

We help manufacturing organizations implement ISO-based quality management systems including ISO 9001 (Quality), ISO 14001 (Environment), and ISO 45001 (Occupational Health & Safety), enabling organizations to enhance product quality and operational efficiency.

Food Industry

For food manufacturers and processors, we provide comprehensive consulting for FSSAI compliance and ISO 22000 (Food Safety Management Systems) certification, ensuring you meet national and international hygiene and quality benchmarks.

Service Industry

For service-based organizations, we support the development of structured quality management processes, operational documentation systems, and continuous improvement frameworks designed to enhance customer satisfaction and build sustainable organizational performance.
